About Us

The Best Holidays Start Here!

Embark on a tranquil journey at our Kingsukh Guest House, enveloped by the scenic allure of Biharinath Hill, Baranti Hill, Staurira Hill, Joychandi Hill, Garipanchkot, Baranti Dam, Malthan Dam, and the captivating Fletcher Dam. Based in the embrace of comfort, relish delightful meals, and unwind in our own verdant garden. Your quiet retreat becomes a promising blend of nature's beauty and heartfelt hospitality.

Explore the hidden gems of Purulia, creating memories that linger long after your stay.
